Challenges


Do a deep breathing exercise.

Breathing exercises can help you relax, because they make your body feel like it does when you are already relaxed. Deep breathing is one of the best ways to lower stress in the body. This is because when you breathe deeply, it sends a message to your brain to calm down and relax.

Do 30 minutes of yoga.

People who took twice-weekly yoga classes experienced a bigger decrease in chronic stress compared to a control group put on a waitlist, found a 2016 study published in the journal Cogent Psychology.

Plan a night in with friends.

Whether it's book club or a Bachelor viewing party, you'll save money versus dinner out (and you can wear comfy clothes).

Call your parents.

Call your parents and say you love them.

Write down something good that happened today.

Even if you've just had the worst day, jot down what you're grateful for instead.

Meet your floormate.

New meetings are very important.
